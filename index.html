Explore Cities in the States

Are you planning a trip to the U.S.? Check out some cities that worth visiting!

By WanYu Yao
April 2025

From the Statue of Liberty to Mount Rushmore, from the Golden Gate Bridge to the Hollywood Sign, the U.S. boasts countless iconic landmarks for visitors to explore. While many tourists love to visit these famous attractions, other cities also offer hidden gems worth exploring. This article introduces four cities that might not be the first choices when planning a trip, but definitely deserve a spot on your travel list.

Ready to discover your next favorite place in the U.S.?

Seattle

Seattle, located on the West Coast in Washington State, is considered the largest city in the Pacific Northwest. As of July 2023, the estimated population of Seattle is 755,078 (U.S. Census Bureau), with a rich diversity of ethnic groups and a vibrant LGBTQ community. One of the most famous coffee chains, Starbucks, originated here — you definitely don’t want to miss visiting the first-ever Starbucks store. Other beloved local coffee brands include Seattle’s Best, Cloud City Coffee, and Victrola Coffee.

Austin

Austin, not only the capital of Texas but also known as the Live Music Capital of the World, provides a unique and charming music scene. Jay Brakefield, author of Texas Music: Its Roots, Its Evolution, pointed out that the "outlaw country" movement of the 1970s — a fusion of country and rock music rebelling against the Nashville sound — flourished in Austin. Beyond country music, Austin’s musical heritage also incorporates rock, blues, soul, folk, electronic and dance music, making it a melting pot of styles.

Chicago

Chicago, the largest city in the Midwest, is renowned for its stunning skyline and lakefront. Be sure to sign up for the River Architecture Tour — as you hop on a vessel for a 90-minute guided cruise, you’ll enjoy breathtaking views of the Chicago skyline and its spectacular architecture. By sitting back on a boat ride along the Chicago River and Lake Michigan, you’ll gain a deeper understanding of the city's rich history. And don't forget to bring a jacket when visiting the "Windy City" — the breeze can be chilly!

Miami

Miami, with its gorgeous weather and beautiful beaches, fulfills our ideal dreams of a perfect vacation. Holding a glass of tequila, basking in the sun or diving into the refreshing waters of South Beach will melt away all the stresses of daily life. If you want to experience the city's artistic vibe, the Bass Museum of Art offers fantastic exhibitions of international contemporary art. Looking for a taste of Latin culture? Head to Little Havana, where food trucks and lively streets will immerse you in an authentic and vibrant atmosphere!
